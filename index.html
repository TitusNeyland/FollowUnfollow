Instagram Follow Back Checker

See who follows you back and who doesn’t. Your data never leaves your device.

How to get your data

  1. In the Instagram app or on instagram.com: Settings → Accounts Center → Your information and permissions → Download your information.
  2. Choose Some of your information, then under Connections select Followers and following.
  3. Format: JSON, date range: All time, then create the download.
  4. When ready, open the ZIP and go to connections → followers_and_following. You’ll see files like followers_1.json and following_1.json.
